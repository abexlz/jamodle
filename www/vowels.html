Vowel Practice

Learn horizontal, vertical, and compound vowels

Korean vowels sit beside or under the consonant in a syllable block. Tap each group to learn the shapes you will see in Hangul Builder and Korean Match.

Vertical vowels

Written beside the consonant — ㅏ, ㅓ, ㅣ

Horizontal vowels

Written under the consonant — ㅗ, ㅜ, ㅡ

Compound vowels

Two vowels combined — ㅘ, ㅝ, ㅢ
